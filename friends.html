Ode to Friends

"There is nothing better than being someone's friend." The theme of friendship has defined spring/summer of 2020, and my friends constantly fill me with wonder. Their diversity of interests reminds me of the vastness of human thought; their various ways of expressing love reminds me of the depth of the human spirit. Just as musical intervals carry unique energies, these friends each contribute a special, distinctive feeling. The acoustic guitar music that follows is inspired by this analogy. Each track starts with the sound of the interval.

- Chirag
