Ode to Friends

"There is nothing better than being someone's friend." The theme of friendship has defined spring/summer of 2020, and my friends constantly fill me with wonder. Their diversity of interests reminds me of the vastness of human thought; their various ways of expressing love reminds me of the depth of the human spirit. Together, they constitute a sort of "complete set," an orthonormal basis, an complete octet...a musical scale. Just as musical intervals bring their own unique energies, these friends each contribute a special, unique feeling. The instrumental acoustic guitar music that follows is inspired by this analogy.
